OUR STORY

"We bake the way our grandmothers did. By feel, not by formula."

Marwan and David grew up surrounded by the generous tables of the Mediterranean, where bread was not merely a vessel, but the foundation of community. Seeking to capture that enduring warmth, they turned to a tradition far from home.

Inspired by rugged northern climates, they began baking a Norwegian-style crispbread in small, demanding batches in Lebanon. Today, these uncompromisingly crisp, seed-dense crackers are broken over tables from Beirut to Dubai to London, honoring the slow, deliberate pace of true craft.
